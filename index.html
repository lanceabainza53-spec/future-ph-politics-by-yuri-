Introduction

Why this archive exists

This site is built as a scrollable public memory wall. It does not say every accused person is guilty. It separates convictions, charges, audit findings, asset recovery records, and ongoing investigations, because legal accuracy matters even when politics is acting like a badly written teleserye. Fuckyou marcos.

Body

The pattern: money moves faster than accountability

Philippine corruption stories often repeat the same structure: public funds are approved for development, the paperwork looks official, middlemen or friendly contractors appear, the project becomes weak, delayed, fake, overpriced, or invisible, and citizens are told to be patient. Very adorable system, if your hobby is losing tax money.

The point of this website is to make the record readable: who was convicted, who was charged, what institution handled the case, and what kind of public spending was involved.
